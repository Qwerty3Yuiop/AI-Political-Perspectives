Summary from the AllSides News Team

On Saturday, Former President Donald Trump held a rally in Texas and raised the prospect of pardons for supporters who participated in the riot at the U.S. Capitol on January 6 if he were to run and win in 2024. 

Trump also issued a warning to prosecutors who he believes are treating the rioters unfairly and continuing to go after him in an attempt to discover unspecified crimes relating to the riot and effort to discredit the result of the 2020 election.  Some left-rated media framed the remarks as vengeful and dangerous; some right-rated media cited the many attempts by prosecutors to investigate Trump since before he took office, framing their efforts as highly politicized and partisan.
