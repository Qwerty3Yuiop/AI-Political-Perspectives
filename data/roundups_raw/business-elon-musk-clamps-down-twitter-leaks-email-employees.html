Summary from the AllSides News Team

Twitter owner and CEO Elon Musk is attempting to clamp down on leaks from inside Twitter HQ. In an email obtained by Platformer (Not Rated) reporter Zoë Schiffer, Musk threatened employees with lawsuits should they violate their non-disclosure agreements.

Key Quotes: The email stated, “as evidenced by the many detailed leaks of confidential Twitter information, a few people at our company continue to act in a manner contrary to the company’s interests and in violation of their NDA. This will be said only once: If you clearly and deliberately violate the NDA that you signed when you joined, you accept liability to the full extent of the law & Twitter will immediately seek damages.”

For Context: Turmoil at Twitter HQ has been a public spectacle since Musk purchased the social media giant in October, as some veteran Twitter employees have been outspoken in their opposition to their new boss. Recently, Musk has been releasing internal information and employee correspondence regarding the platform’s censorship and moderation practices in the lead up to the 2020 election and the aftermath of January 6th.

How the Media is Covering It: The email is being covered across the spectrum. It’s the biggest story regarding Twitter on the left. The right is more focused on recent entries in the ‘Twitter Files’ saga, including internal discussions on banning former President Donald Trump from the platform, which the left is not covering as heavily.
