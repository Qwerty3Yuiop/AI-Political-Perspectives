Summary from the AllSides News Team

The House select committee investigating the January 6 Capitol riot has subpoenaed Jeffrey Clark, a former Justice Department official under President Donald Trump who was involved in questioning the results of the 2020 election. Clark was found to have drafted a proposal to intercede in the Georgia certification process, and he questioned the results in other states.

Clark is the latest of several Trump advisors and allies who have been subpoenaed by the panel.  Among those previously subpoenaed is former Trump advisor Steve Bannon, who is refusing to testify.
