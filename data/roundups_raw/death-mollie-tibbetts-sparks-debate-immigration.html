Summary from the AllSides News Team

The death of University of Iowa student Mollie Tibbetts has reignited a national debate on illegal immigration after an undocumented immigrant was arrested for her murder. Many on the Right have said that the crime is an example of the country's failed immigration policy, while some on the Left have criticized the president for politicizing the event and point to multiple studies that claim illegal immigrants do not commit crimes at higher rates than native-born U.S. citizens.
