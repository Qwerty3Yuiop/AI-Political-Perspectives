Summary from the AllSides News Team

An armed California man was arrested early Wednesday morning near Supreme Court Justice Brett Kavanaugh’s Maryland home, several reports said. 

A Supreme Court spokesperson said the man was apprehended around 1:50 a.m. after telling police officers he intended to kill Kavanaugh; police reportedly visited the neighborhood after receiving an anonymous tip about the man. Sources cited by The Washington Post (Lean Left bias), which first reported the story, said the man was angry about recent mass shootings and the leaked draft Supreme Court opinion that would overturn Roe v. Wade. While it was unclear on Wednesday afternoon what type of gun the man had, sources told Fox News (Right bias) the man also had a knife. 

Coverage was widespread across the spectrum on Wednesday afternoon, although right-rated outlets featured the story much more prominently on homepages than left-rated outlets. Coverage across the spectrum tied the news to recent DHS warnings of potential threats against Supreme Court justices. Right-rated outlets often framed the incident as an attack and tied it to recent protests outside of some justices’ homes; New York Post (Lean Right bias) featured the story with the homepage tagline, “ENEMY AT THE GATES.” Update 6/8/22 1:50 p.m.: Previous version implied "officials" said the man was from California; while sources had told this to several outlets, officials had not yet confirmed this publicly.
