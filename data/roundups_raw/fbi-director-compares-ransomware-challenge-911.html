Summary from the AllSides News Team

In a Thursday interview with The Wall Street Journal, FBI Director Christopher Wray compared the recent wave of ransomware cyberattacks to the challenge posed by the 9/11 terrorist attacks. “The scale of this problem is one that I think the country has to come to terms with,” Wray said. The same day, Reuters reported that the Department of Justice would treat ransomware attacks with the same priority as terrorism, directing U.S. attorney’s offices to coordinate with a recently created task force in Washington, D.C. Additionally, the White House released an open letter urging businesses to take security measures against ransomware attacks.

Coverage was generally balanced across the spectrum and split between Wray’s comments, the Reuters report and the White House letter.
