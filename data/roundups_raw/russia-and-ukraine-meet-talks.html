Summary from the AllSides News Team

Envoys from Russia and Ukraine meet on Monday at the Ukraine-Belarus border amid hopes for a diplomatic breakthrough. 

Ukraine is reportedly seeking an immediate ceasefire. Moscow would not comment on its objectives going into the talks. Expectations are reportedly low after Russia confirmed over the weekend that nuclear forces were on high alert.

The talks come on the fifth day of fighting, as Ukraine resistance continues to slow down the advance of Russian troops on major Ukrainian cities. Meanwhile, sanctions from Western countries directed at the Russian economy seek to cut off Moscow's major financial institutions, and the value of the Russian ruble has fallen 30%. Several NATO countries, including the United States, have also stepped up weapons supplies to Ukraine.
