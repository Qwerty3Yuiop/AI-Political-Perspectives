Summary from the AllSides News Team

President Joe Biden tested positive for the coronavirus on Thursday morning, a White House memo said.

Biden tested positive during routine antigen testing, White House physician Dr. Kevin O’Connor said. The positive test was confirmed with a PCR test. Biden has been fully vaccinated and twice boosted, and the White House confirmed he was taking Paxlovid, Pfizer's antiviral COVID-19 pill.

This is the first time the president has tested positive for the virus, White House press secretary Karine Jean-Pierre said. In a tweet from the @POTUS account, Biden said, “Folks, I'm doing great. Thanks for your concern. Just called Senator Casey, Congressman Cartwright, and Mayor Cognetti (and my Scranton cousins!) to send my regrets for missing our event today. Keeping busy!”

Coverage was widespread and prominently featured across the spectrum. A homepage headline in the New York Post (Lean Right bias) highlighted that Biden was “vaxxed and boosted.” Some headlines from left-rated outlets highlighted that Biden had “mild” symptoms.
