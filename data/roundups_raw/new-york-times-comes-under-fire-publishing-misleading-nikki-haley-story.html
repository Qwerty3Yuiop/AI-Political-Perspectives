Summary from the AllSides News Team

The New York Times has come under fire for publishing a story on Thursday that implied UN Ambassador Nikki Haley spent over $52,000 on curtains for her official State Department residence, when the decor was actually purchased by the Obama administration. The Times has since issued a correction.
