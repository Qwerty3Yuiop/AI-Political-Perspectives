Summary from the AllSides News Team

On Wednesday, former President Donald Trump will go to McAllen, Texas with Texas Gov. Greg Abbott (R) and a group of congressional Republicans to tour an unfinished part of the border wall and to hold a town hall event with supporters.

Trump is expected to renew his attacks on President Joe Biden's immigration policies as the rise in migrants attempting to enter the U.S. at the southern border continues. Since taking office, Biden has reversed many of Trump's immigration policies.
