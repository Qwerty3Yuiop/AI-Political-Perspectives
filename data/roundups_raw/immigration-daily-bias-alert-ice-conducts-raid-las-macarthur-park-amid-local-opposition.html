Summary from the AllSides News Team

Federal agents, accompanied by National Guard troops, conducted an immigration raid in MacArthur Park, Los Angeles, drawing protests and condemnation from local officials and residents.

The Details: The raid targeted a largely immigrant neighborhood reportedly known for its MS-13 influence. Los Angeles Mayor Karen Bass and California Governor Gavin Newsom criticized the operation, with Bass calling it "outrageous" and "un-American" and Newsom describing it as "theater." The mayor questioned the motives behind the operation, noting that the raid disrupted a summer day camp.

For Context: President Donald Trump's administration has been pushing forward with plans to carry out mass deportation operations and has conducted numerous immigration raids, leading to widespread protests and criticism.
