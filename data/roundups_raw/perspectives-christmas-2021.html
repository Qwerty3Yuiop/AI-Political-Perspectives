Summary from the AllSides News Team

On America's second Christmas marked by COVID-19, voices on all sides of politics offered perspectives on the holiday, its meaning, celebrations and public health.

Many viewpoints across the spectrum celebrated Christmas while connecting it to COVID-19. Left-rated voices often focused on overcoming pandemic-induced grief and sadness, protecting the safety of loved ones and conquering social justice issues. Many on the right reflected on Christianity and faith; some condemned fear-mongering and efforts to discourage celebrations, and expressed pity for those who've purportedly let fear of COVID-19 control their lives.
