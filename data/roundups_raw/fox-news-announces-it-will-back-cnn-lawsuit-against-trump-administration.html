Summary from the AllSides News Team

Fox News announced it will back CNN's lawsuit against the Trump administration and will file an amicus brief on their behalf after the White House revoked Jim Acosta's press credentials. "While we don’t condone the growing antagonistic tone by both the president and the press at recent media avails, we do support a free press, access and open exchanges for the American people,” sad Fox News President Jay Wallace.
