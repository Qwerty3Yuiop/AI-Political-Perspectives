Summary from the AllSides News Team

On this Memorial Day, AllSides joins all Americans in celebrating the men and women in the military who have died while serving on duty.

Flags will be flown at half staff Monday morning as Americans decorate the graves of their fallen heroes; the Department of Veterans Affairs has relaxed rules on decorations at the nation's 155 veterans cemeteries.
