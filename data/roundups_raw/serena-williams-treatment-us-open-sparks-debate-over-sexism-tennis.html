Summary from the AllSides News Team

Serena Williams on Saturday lost the U.S. Open final to Naomi Osaka after a series of tense exchanges with the umpire who penalized her for breaking her racket, allegedly receiving mid-match coaching, and calling him a "thief." Some say that the code violations she received were overly harsh and sexist, while others argue that Williams broke the rules and that Osaka is the rightful winner.
