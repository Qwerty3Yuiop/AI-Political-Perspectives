Summary from the AllSides News Team

House Speaker Nancy Pelosi (D-Calif.) has proposed a resolution to form a select committee to investigate the deadly violence at the U.S. Capitol on January 6. The resolution should come to a vote in the House later this week.

The committee would include 13 members: eight chosen by Pelosi, and the other five chosen in consultation with House Minority Leader Kevin McCarthy (R-Calif.). An earlier proposal by Rep. John Katko (R-N.Y.) for equal representation on the committee failed in the Senate.
