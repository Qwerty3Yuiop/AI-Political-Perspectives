Summary from the AllSides News Team

Instagram removed a post from right-wing provocateur Milo Yiannopoulos in which he said that it's "disgusting and sad" that the mail bombs sent to several Democratic figures "didn't go off." The social media platform initially said the post did not violate its community guidelines, but it eventually reversed its decision.
