Summary from the AllSides News Team

House Democrats have demanded that Attorney General William Barr release special counsel Robert Mueller's report to the public. The letter was sent on Friday, which argued that the public should be given a chance to review Mueller's report "without delay and to the maximum extent permitted by law."
