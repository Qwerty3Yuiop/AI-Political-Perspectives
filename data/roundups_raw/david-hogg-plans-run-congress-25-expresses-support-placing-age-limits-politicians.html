Summary from the AllSides News Team

In a profile piece by New York Magazine, Parkland shooting survivor and gun control activist David Hogg says he will run for Congress at age 25, expressing support for placing age limits on politicians. “Older Democrats just won’t move the f— off the plate and let us take control. Nancy Pelosi is old," he said.
