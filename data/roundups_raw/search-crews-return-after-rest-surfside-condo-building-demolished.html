Summary from the AllSides News Team

Search crews will return to the site of the collapsed Miami condo building Monday after the remaining section is demolished. The death toll stands at 24 Monday, and 121 people remain missing.

According to rescuers, the demolition will give them access to parts of the site that they have been unable to search.
