Summary from the AllSides News Team

A Pennsylvania state lawmaker has introduced a bill that would ban public schoolteachers from discussing many modern-day political matters in the classroom and prohibit them from endorsing, supporting, or opposing any candidate running for office. Supporters say it will protect classrooms from bias, while dissenters say it will stifle teachers' free speech.
