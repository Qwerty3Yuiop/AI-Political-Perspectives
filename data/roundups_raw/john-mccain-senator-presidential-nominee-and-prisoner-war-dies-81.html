Summary from the AllSides News Team

Senator John McCain died Saturday night after a battle with brain cancer. He was 81, and immediately received condolences and words of respect from across the aisle. Known throughout his political career as a maverick, he was the 2008 Republican nominee for President and previously a prisoner of war.
