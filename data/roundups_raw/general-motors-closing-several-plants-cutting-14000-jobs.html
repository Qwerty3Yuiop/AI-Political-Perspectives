Summary from the AllSides News Team

General Motors announced it will close five plants across the U.S and Canada and will cut 14,000 jobs, citing declining sales and a desire to focus on innovation. Some have said the news will hurt President Trump, as he has consistently campaigned on bringing jobs back to areas who have been impacted by factory closures.
