Summary from the AllSides News Team

U.S. House Democrats are returning to Washington D.C. Monday to advance President Joe Biden's $3.5 trillion budget plan.  Democratic House leadership plans to approve a "rule" on Monday to set up a vote on Tuesday.

Democrats in the House are currently working to string together votes from nine moderate Democrats who want the $1.2 trillion infrastructure bill recently passed by the Senate to pass the House before taking up the budget package.  Speaker Nancy Pelosi (D) can lose only three of the Senate's 50 Democratic votes before the $3.5 trillion bill is defeated.
