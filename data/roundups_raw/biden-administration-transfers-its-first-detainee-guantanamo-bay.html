Summary from the AllSides News Team

On Monday morning, the Department of Defense announced the transfer of a detainee from Guantanamo Bay to his home country of Morocco. The move represented a policy change from the Trump administration as President Biden is reported to be starting efforts to close the detention facility.

The facility was opened under former President George W. Bush in 2002 after the 9/11 attacks.
