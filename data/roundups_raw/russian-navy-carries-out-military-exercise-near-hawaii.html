Summary from the AllSides News Team

The Russian Navy is conducting military maneuver exercises near Hawaii as President Joe Biden meets with Russian President Vladimir Putin in Geneva.  The U.S. responded by scrambling three F-22 stealth fighter jets from Hawaii.

According to Russian officials, the exercise is Russia's largest maneuver in the Pacific Ocean since the end of the Cold War.
