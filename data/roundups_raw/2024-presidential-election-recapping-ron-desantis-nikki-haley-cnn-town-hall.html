Summary from the AllSides News Team

2024 Republican presidential candidates Ron DeSantis and Nikki Haley participated in a town hall event on CNN (Lean Left bias) in Iowa Thursday night.

DeSantis: DeSantis discussed his abortion stance and contrasted it with that of former President Donald Trump, saying, "For pro-life voters in Iowa, Donald Trump is taking positions that are way different than what he professed to believe when he first ran for president." He also discussed guns in the wake of the Iowa school shooting and mentioned his handling of the Parkland shooting in 2018.

Haley: Haley focused on issues like guns and mental health, while also remarking on her controversial remarks about the cause of the Civil War, saying she "should have said slavery right off the bat." She also criticized Trump, saying the country "won't survive" another Trump presidency.

How the Media Covered It: Sources across the spectrum covered the event. Many highlighted the candidates' criticisms of Trump and each other.
