Summary from the AllSides News Team

Former Senator John Warner, who represented Virginia in the U.S. Senate for three decades, has died at age 94.  He served as Chairman of the Senate Armed Services Committee and played a central role in shaping military affairs legislation.

Warner served in the U.S. Navy during World War II and the Marines during the Korean War. He later served as secretary of the Navy and as a Republican U.S. senator from 1979 through 2009.  He was also married to actress Elizabeth Taylor from 1976 to 1982.
