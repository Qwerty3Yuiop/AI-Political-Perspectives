Summary from the AllSides News Team

The Trump administration asked the Supreme Court on Friday, to issue a "quick" ruling on President Trump's policy that bars most transgender individuals from military service. The policy was initially announced by President Trump via Twitter in 2017 and later officially released by Secretary of Defense, James Mattis.
