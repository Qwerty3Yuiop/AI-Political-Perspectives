Summary from the AllSides News Team

Actor Jussie Smollett claims he was attacked last month by two men who hurled racial and homophobic slurs and yelled "MAGA country." Many prominent Democrats, including presidential contenders, were quick to blame the incident on rising hostility cultivated by Trump and his supporters. But after more details emerged revealing the incident may be a hoax created by Smollett himself, some are raising concerns about bias.
