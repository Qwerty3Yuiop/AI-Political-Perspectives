Summary from the AllSides News Team

Fox News rebuked hosts Sean Hannity and Jeanine Pirro after they appeared on stage at President Trump's Missouri rally on Monday, with the network calling it "an unfortunate distraction" that "has been addressed.” During his speech, Hannity pointed to the media holding area and said, “All those people in the back are fake news.”
