Summary from the AllSides News Team

Democratic Sen. Cory Booker unveiled a new bill that would create taxpayer funded savings accounts for every American child, with the federal government depositing $1,000 at birth and giving up to an additional $2,000 a year based on family income until the recipient turns 18.
