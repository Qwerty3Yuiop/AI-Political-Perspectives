Summary from the AllSides News Team

NBC News on Sunday issued a correction on Twitter after the network erroneously claimed that President Trump called Robert E. Lee "incredible" when he was actually referring to Ulysses S. Grant. In context, Trump said that Lee was “a true great fighter and a great general," but the comments were part of a larger discussion about Grant's exceptional skill in defeating him.
