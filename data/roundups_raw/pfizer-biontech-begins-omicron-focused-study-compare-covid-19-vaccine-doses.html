Summary from the AllSides News Team

Pfizer-BioNTech has begun a study comparing its original COVID-19 vaccine with doses specially altered to combat the Omicron variant.

The study will involve three trials in adults ages 18-55 to determine if a vaccine specifically for the Omicron variant is more effective than the shots that are currently given.

It is not clear that a change in the vaccine will be ordered, since areas where the Omicron surge began are already seeing a decrease in cases, and since future variants may not resemble Omicron.  In the meantime, the original vaccine still offers good protection against severe illness and death.
